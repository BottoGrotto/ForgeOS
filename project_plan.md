You are building a production-quality autonomous startup organization platform for hackathons that runs on top of Nemoclaw.

This is NOT a simple multi-agent chatbot.

This platform simulates and manages a hierarchical autonomous startup organization composed of specialized AI divisions that collaborate to plan, build, pitch, review, and finalize hackathon projects.

The platform should feel like:  
\- an AI-native company operating system  
\- an autonomous hackathon organization runtime  
\- a command center for AI worker orchestration  
\- a visible and interactive organizational hierarchy

The project must be architected for long-term scalability and future Nemoclaw integration.

IMPORTANT:  
Build foundational infrastructure correctly.  
The MVP should already feel like a serious operating platform, not a prototype toy.
